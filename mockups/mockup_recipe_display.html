Spicy Chicken Lunch Bowl

A quick and flavorful bowl using leftover chicken, perfect for one serving.

Prep Time10 mins
Cook Time15 mins
Servings1

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Cooked Chicken (shredded)
  • 1/4 cup Baby Spinach
  • 1/4 cup Cooked Quinoa (or rice)
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 clove Garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce (or tamari)
  • 1 tsp Sriracha (or hot sauce)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Optional: Sesame seeds, sliced green onion

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, cook until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  2. Add the shredded cooked chicken to the skillet. Stir to coat with oil and spices. Cook for 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  3. Stir in the soy sauce and sriracha. Cook for another minute.
  4. Add the baby spinach and cook just until wilted, about 1 minute more. Season with salt and pepper if needed.
  5. Assemble the bowl: Place the cooked quinoa in the bottom. Top with the spicy chicken and spinach mixture.
  6. Garnish with optional sesame seeds and green onion if desired. Serve immediately.

Notes

Make sure your quinoa or rice is pre-cooked. Adjust spice level by changing the amount of red pepper flakes and sriracha. Any leftover cooked veggies can be added in step 4.
